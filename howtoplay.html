How to Play?

The current status of your character is show by each bar. Overtime, the bar will get lower. To increase it, click the button next to the bar. Each bar also affects other bars (as seen by the yellow bar), so make sure it's balanced. There's a time jump when you increase acivities. You will earn money every second (one second represents one minute in the game) and certain action requires money. Use your money wisely or else you will be bankrupt! You can also choose to have a partner, but beware having a partner is not always a good choice in college life. You also have to keep studying, otherwise you will get dropped out. The simulation will end if your character is ravenous, is sleep deprived, goes broke, or is stressed out. To finish the simulation, you have to keep studying until you pass semester 8, just like in real life.
